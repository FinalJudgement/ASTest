Experience

We've been in the business since 2002 and have worked on hundreds of machines including pinball, redemption, video games, crane games, and carnival style games. We provide customers with exceptional and timely service to ensure our customers' satisfaction. We firmly believe in the restoration, preservation, and longevity of the vintage arcade and pinball games that have paved the road to the success of modern arcades.

Passion

For us, arcade and pinball machines aren't just a business, but our passion. Based in Huntington, New York, in long island, we've spent countless years upgrading, customizing, and repairing arcade equipment. We know what it takes to get the job done right, and we strive to ensure that your arcade and pinball machines receive the proper repairs they need so that you can get back to enjoying the game.
